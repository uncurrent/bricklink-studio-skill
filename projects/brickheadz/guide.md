Build Brickheadz-style LEGO figures: oversized blocky head on a minimal body,

## Constraints

*(To be filled in when work begins)*

Key constraints specific to Brickheadz format:
- Head is typically 6×6×6 studs (or similar blocky proportions)
- Body is minimal (4–6 studs wide, 4 tall)
- Legs: 2-stud wide each
- Official sets use specific part combinations for facial features
